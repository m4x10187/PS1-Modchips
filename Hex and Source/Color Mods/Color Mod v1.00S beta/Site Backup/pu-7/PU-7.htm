PU-7 Board

Top Side of PC board

 

Wire configuration.

1---Side of chip
2---Pin 6 , counting from the top right edge of the IC.
3---Solder to the top of the very small IC. Take care not to solder to any other component.
4---Not used. Cut excess wire off and insulate to prevent shorting.

Bottom Side of the PC board.

Legend.

A---Cut the data trace with a sharp stanley knife between the small chip and the solder pad.

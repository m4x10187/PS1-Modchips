PU-8 Board

Bottom of PU-8 PC board - Fig A

Figure A


Cut the PC board with a sharp stanley knife in the two positions shown. The aim is to cut the two data traces on the PC board.
Tip: Twist the knife into the Board, this will help to get it started.

Top of PU-8 PC Board - Fig B

Figure B

Wire Configuration

1---Solder to side of the IC chip.
2---Solder to pin 6 of the IC chip, counting from lower left edge of the IC.
3---Solder to the first pin on the top left edge of the IC chip.
4---Not used. Cut excess wire and insulate from shorting.

Close up of PC board modification - FIG C

At white arrow, scrape the resin (soldermask) off the PC board, and join the small eye at the end of the data trace, to the main plate with a dab of solder.

Note : Make sure you join the correct data trace. TIP: It's the one that runs from PIN 7, next to wire 2.

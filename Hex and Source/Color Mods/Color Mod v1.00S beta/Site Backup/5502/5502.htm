5502 Board

Top Side of 5502 PC board

Wire Configuration.

1---Solder to the side of the IC (actually an SMD ferrite bead).

Bottom Side of the 5502 PC board

Wire Configuration.

4---Solder to IC pin 1, counting from the lower right edge of the IC.
2---De-solder the leg of pin 6 of the IC, and solder the wire to the leg of the IC.
3---De-solder the leg of Pin 7 of the IC, and solder the wire to the leg of the IC.

Important note: Wires 2 and 3 must be soldered to the legs of the IC. The legs of the IC (Pin 6 and 7, counting from the lower right edge of the IC) are de-soldered from the main PC baord, and gently lifted up away from the board (use a sharp Stanley knife to lever the legs up). It is important to leave these legs off the main PC board and solder the wires to these legs.

Position of the Colour chip on the 5502

Place the colour chip at the far left edge on top of the steel plate. Make sure you do not clamp the wires under the steel plate when you tighten down the screws.
